About the school

Education that prepares children for marks, manners and modern life.

Gandhi Memorial English High School nurtures students through purposeful teaching, personal attention and a school culture built on respect. Children are encouraged to think clearly, communicate confidently and grow into responsible young citizens.

Along with classroom learning, the school supports sports, cultural programs, science activities, leadership opportunities and value-based development.
